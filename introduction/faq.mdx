<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long do points stay valid?">
    Customer points expire 24 months from the earning date.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which currency does Points use?">
    SAR. All amounts in the API are in Saudi Riyals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can one customer redeem at multiple merchants?">
    Yes — the Points wallet is unified across the whole merchant network. Any balance earned at one merchant can be redeemed at any other participating merchant.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need both Earn-only and the full Checkout?">
    No — pick whichever matches your business. You can start with [Earn-only](/integration/earn-only) and upgrade to the full [Checkout flow](/integration/checkout-flow) later.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a sandbox?">
    Yes. Email [support@papp.sa](mailto:support@papp.sa) for sandbox credentials.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
