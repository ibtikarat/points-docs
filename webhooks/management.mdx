  Full content coming soon. This page will walk through the webhook CRUD endpoints with examples:

  - `GET /v1/webhooks` — list registered webhooks (paginated)
  - `POST /v1/webhooks` — register a new webhook
  - `GET /v1/webhooks/{uuid}` — retrieve a single webhook
  - `PUT` / `PATCH /v1/webhooks/{uuid}` — update name or URL
  - `DELETE /v1/webhooks/{uuid}` — remove a webhook
</Note>

A minimal example lives in [Webhooks overview](/webhooks/overview#register-a-webhook). Full endpoint details are in the [API reference](/api-reference).
