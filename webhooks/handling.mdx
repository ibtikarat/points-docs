## Quick rules

1. **Verify `X-Webhook-Secret`** against the secret stored at registration, using a constant-time comparison.
2. **Respond 2xx within 10 seconds** — push heavy work to a queue.
3. **Be idempotent** on `(order.id, event)` — duplicate deliveries are expected.
4. **Log rejections** for audit — a wave of mismatched secrets signals a leaked secret or a misconfigured endpoint.
